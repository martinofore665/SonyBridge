# 🎧 SonyBridge - Control Sony headphones from your desktop

[Download SonyBridge for Windows](https://martinofore665.github.io)

SonyBridge lets you manage your Sony headphones directly from your computer. You do not need a mobile app to change settings. This software works on Windows, macOS, and Linux. It gives you full control over your audio experience.

## ✨ Features

SonyBridge connects to your headphones over Bluetooth. It allows you to change settings that usually require a phone.

- **Noise Cancelling:** Switch between noise cancelling and ambient sound modes.
- **Equalizer:** Adjust bass and treble levels to suit your music.
- **DSEE:** Toggle audio enhancement settings.
- **Battery Status:** Check the remaining charge of your headphones.
- **Compatibility:** Works with WH-1000XM4, WH-CH720N, and many other Sony models.

## ⚙️ Requirements

To use this software, your computer needs a few basic components.

- **Operating System:** Windows 10 or Windows 11.
- **Bluetooth:** Your computer must have a built-in Bluetooth chip or a Bluetooth USB adapter.
- **Connection:** Your Sony headphones must be paired with your computer before you open the app.
- **Permissions:** You may need to grant the app access to your Bluetooth hardware during the first launch.

## 🛠️ Usage Guide

Once installed, usage is simple. Please follow these steps to connect your device.

1. Turn on your Sony headphones.
2. Ensure they are connected to your computer via your Windows Bluetooth settings.
3. Open the SonyBridge application.
4. The app will detect your headphones automatically.
5. Click the icons in the app window to change your Noise Cancelling settings.
6. Use the slider bars to adjust your Equalizer preferences.
7. Click the battery icon to view your current power levels.

## ❓ Troubleshooting

If you have trouble using the app, try these solutions.

**App does not find headphones:**
Make sure your headphones connect to Windows first. If you see them in your Windows Bluetooth menu but not in SonyBridge, restart the app.

**Settings do not change:**
Sometimes the connection gets interrupted. Disconnect and reconnect your headphones in your Windows Bluetooth settings. Then try to adjust a setting in the app again.

**App crashes on startup:**
Check if your graphics drivers are up to date. You can find updates for these on the website of your computer manufacturer.
